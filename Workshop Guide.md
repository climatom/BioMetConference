# BioMet Conference Workshop
## Participant Guide

Welcome! This repository accompanies the BioMet Conference workshop on **Humid Heat Metrics and Impact-Based Forecasting**.

The workshop consists of two self-contained notebooks. We will work through them together during the session, but they can also be completed independently afterwards. 
Ideally participants will get through both notebooks, but they are relatively self-contained and you might prefer a deeper-dive on just one. 
See below for a very brief overview of what's in each notebook. 

# Choosing a notebook

## 📘 Notebook 1 — Humid Heat Metrics

This notebook focuses on understanding and analysing humid heat.

It is aimed at participants interested in:

- climate and weather analysis
- reanalysis datasets
- understanding humid heat metrics
- the physical basis of thermal stress

It walks through a case study of the June 2026 heatwave in the UK

## 📗 Notebook 2 — Real-Time Impact-Based Forecasting

This notebook focuses on operational forecasting using the latest ECMWF forecasts.

It is aimed at participants interested in:

- computation of humid heat metrics
- thermal physiology
- impact-based forecasting

It explores a forecasting system for predicting 'unsurvivable' heat.
